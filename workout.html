🏋️ Weekly Workout Plan

Monday

Chest & Triceps

  • Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets x 10–12 reps
  • Cable Flyes – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extension – 3 sets x 10 reps

Tuesday

Back & Biceps

  • Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Barbell Rows – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Hammer Curls – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • EZ-Bar Curls – 3 sets x 10 reps

Wednesday

Rest Day

  • Take it easy! Focus on recovery.
  • Stretching, walking, or yoga is optional.

Thursday

Legs

  • Squats – 5 sets x 6–8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Leg Press – 4 sets x 10–12 reps
  • Walking Lunges – 3 sets x 12 steps per leg
  • Standing Calf Raises – 4 sets x 15 reps

Friday

Shoulders & Abs

  • Overhead Press – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Front Raises – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3 sets x 12–15 reps

Saturday

Rest Day

  • Active recovery: walk, swim, or relax.
  • Focus on hydration and nutrition.

Sunday

Full Body or Core

  • Deadlifts – 4 sets x 6–8 reps
  • Kettlebell Swings – 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Plank – 3 sets x 60 seconds
  • Push-Ups – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Russian Twists – 3 sets x 20 reps
